French Toast

Description

Chicken Saute is one of the favorite main dishes of Turkish cuisine.

It is very easy and economical to prepare. It is made with chicken breast and many other ingredients that can add different flavors to the dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breast
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 orange bell pepper
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 clove garlic
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 5 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions

  1. Put the olive oil to the saucepan and then add the cubed cut chicken breasts. Saute them at medium heat about 3 minutes. Add sliced onions, garlic and continue to saute all until onions get softy.
  2. Add the julienne cut red, orange and green sweet peppers, ground paprika and saute them about 7 minutes until sweet peppers get softy.
  3. Add the soy sauce and continue to stir all about 2-3 minutes. Then add the 1/2 cup of water and close the saucepan lid and let it cook 20 minutes at low-medium heat.
